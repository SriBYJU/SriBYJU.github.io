About This Project

This website teaches advanced dinking and at-the-line offensive techniques for high-level pickleball players. It reflects my personal journey toward becoming a professional player while helping others improve their skills.

The platform includes written explanations, visual breakdowns, and video demonstrations of advanced dinking strategies. It focuses on helping players improve control, precision, and offensive awareness at the kitchen line.

Advanced Dinking Techniques

  • Soft Hands Control: Maintaining a loose grip to control the paddle and ball height.
  • Paddle Angle Awareness: Adjusting face angle to keep dinks low and unattackable.
  • Push Dinks & Attack Setups: Using soft but intentional pressure to move opponents and set up offensive shots.
  • Resets at the Kitchen: Recovering from tough positions to regain offensive opportunities.
  • Footwork & Positioning: Moving efficiently to stay balanced and ready for all shots.
  • Disguising Shots: Making your intent unclear to opponents until the last moment.
