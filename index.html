About This Project

I chose to focus on advanced dinking and at-the-line offensive techniques because pickleball is personal to me. My dream is to go professional, and I am currently on that path.

This website is designed to help other players reach their goals while also strengthening my own understanding of high-level pickleball. By sharing techniques I’ve learned and developed, I hope to make the journey smoother for other competitive and aspiring professional players.

Advanced Dinking Techniques

  • Soft Hands Control: Using a relaxed grip to maintain consistency and touch.
  • Paddle Angle Awareness: Adjusting face angle to keep dinks low and unattackable.
  • Push Dinks: Applying light pressure to move opponents out of position.
  • Attack Setups: Disguising offensive intentions during dink exchanges.
  • Resets at the Kitchen: Recovering from tough positions with controlled drops.
